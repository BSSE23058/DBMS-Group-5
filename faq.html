Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SkillConnect

How do I sign up as a tutor on SkillConnect?

To sign up as a tutor, click on the "Student / Tutor Registration" link in the Register menu, fill out the required information, and submit your application. Our team will review your details and notify you once your account is approved.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for payments.

How does the refund policy work?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all courses. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, simply contact our support team within 30 days for a full refund.

Can I access courses on mobile devices?

Yes! SkillConnect is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets through our mobile app or web browser.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team 24/7 through the contact form on our website, or by emailing support@skillconnect.com. Average response time is under 2 hours.

How do employers register and post jobs?

Employers can register by selecting "Employer Registration" from the Register menu. Once registered and logged in, you can post job openings and manage applications from your dashboard.

How do job seekers apply for jobs?

Job seekers should register using the "Job Seeker Registration" form. After logging in, you can browse available jobs and apply directly through the platform.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your data and never share your personal information without your consent.

Can I register as both a tutor and a job seeker?

Yes, you can register for multiple roles using the appropriate registration forms. Each role will have its own dashboard and features.
